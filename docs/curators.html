Wing IV · Meet the Curators

The keepers of this hall.

Every museum has its custodians. Ours are not from here. They are three of Earth's nearest neighbours, each with their own perspective on what disaster means, what it costs, and what it leaves behind.

I · The Archivist

Moon ☾

Keeper of Records · Resident, Earth Orbit

I have watched every storm. I have watched every quake. From up here, fifty years is barely a breath — but each entry in this archive is mine to keep, and yours to read.

A note on this exhibit

The three voices above are imagined, of course. They speak for us — the team that built this museum — and for the perspective each celestial body would offer if it could. The data, however, is real. Every figure in every other wing of this museum is sourced from the EM-DAT International Disaster Database.
